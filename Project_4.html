ABOUT THE PROJECT

This analysis examines delivery operations and customer experience using logistics data from Three Logistics Company. The goal was to identify patterns in delivery delays, fleet utilization, and customer satisfaction, and assess their effects on operational efficiency and costs. By analyzing delivery times, truck usage, route distances, and customer feedback, the project highlighted inefficiencies and provided data-driven recommendations to enhance operational workflows and improve the quality of customer service, ultimately aiming to boost the company’s overall efficiency and profitability.

KEY ACTIVITIES PERFORMED

  • Cleaned and prepared the dataset using Microsoft Excel by resolving data inconsistencies, formatting issues, and filling missing values. Applied statistical approaches for data imputation, standardized formats for consistency, and used Excel functions to identify and correct gaps, ensuring the dataset was accurate and reliable for analysis.
  • Employed exploratory data analysis (EDA) techniques to gain a deep understanding of the data’s nature and distribution. Utilized visualizations and summary statistics to uncover patterns, trends, and anomalies. Examined relationships and correlations between variables to identify key drivers and dependencies, supporting more informed analysis and decision-making.
  • Analyzed delivery times, truck utilization, and route distances to identify operational bottlenecks and underutilized resources, uncovering key inefficiencies.
  • Investigated client satisfaction scores alongside delivery delays and route distances to evaluate how logistics performance impacts customer experience and satisfaction.
  • Developed key performance indicators (KPIs) including average delivery time, on-time delivery rate, and number of delivery trips to quantitatively measure operational performance and efficiency. These metrics enabled identification of bottlenecks, evaluation of fleet utilization, and tracking of service reliability.
  • Designed interactive Power BI dashboards incorporating dynamic filters, slicers, and drill-down features to visualize and analyze trends in delivery delays, fleet performance, and customer satisfaction. The dashboards provided stakeholders with clear, actionable insights, supporting data-driven decisions to optimize logistics operations and improve customer service outcomes.
  • Delivered actionable insights and strategic recommendations to optimize delivery speed, improve fleet management, and elevate customer satisfaction.
  • Communicated findings clearly to stakeholders, supporting data-driven decision making and enabling operational improvements.
