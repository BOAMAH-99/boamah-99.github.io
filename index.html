Data Collection & Preparation

I collect and manage end-to-end data workflows, including data sourcing, extraction, and wrangling. Using SQL, Excel, and Python, I clean, validate, and transform raw data into structured formats optimized for analysis and automation. Analysis & Interpretation

I focus on uncovering patterns, measuring impact, and deriving actionable insights. I leverage Python libraries and Excel to perform descriptive and inferential statistical analyses, and use SQL to execute queries across large-scale relational databases.

Communication & presentation
