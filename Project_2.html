ABOUT THE PROJECT

This project focused on analyzing customer financial and demographic data to segment clients based on their credit risk levels. Using Excel for data cleaning and transformation, and Power BI for visualization, the analysis identified patterns and characteristics linked to higher default rates.

SKILLS

- Analyzed and prepared customer data with demographics, credit, and transaction details. - Segmented customers by credit score, credit utilization, employment, and debt-to-income ratios to identify risk groups. - Created calculated metrics (e.g., Customer Risk Tier) for effective segmentation. - Applied credit risk knowledge to interpret default patterns and key risk drivers. - Designed clear visualizations; heatmaps, risk matrices, and KPI cards to highlight high-risk segments. - Used Power BI for interactive reporting with filters. - Integrated customer lifetime value (CLV) to prioritize high-risk, high-value customers. - Developed actionable business insights and recommendations for risk mitigation and credit management. - Communicated findings effectively to support decision-making and executive understanding.
