ABOUT THE PROJECT

This project developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to segment customers based on risk profiles, utilization ratios, and payment history. Starting with thorough data analysis and preparation, it established calculated metrics like Customer Risk Tier to classify customers into meaningful risk groups. The analysis identified high-risk segments, early-warning indicators of default, and opportunities for targeted retention. The project delivered actionable insights discovered and recommendations for reducing default rate while maximizing value from moderate-risk customers

KEY ACTIVITIES PERFORMED

  • I analyzed and prepared the customer dataset using data preparation techniques to ensure accuracy and reliability for analysis. I segmented customers into distinct risk groups to identify those requiring closer attention due to their higher default risk. By creating calculated metrics like the Customer Risk Tier, I enhanced the precision and effectiveness of these segments.
  • I designed clear, intuitive visualizations including; heatmaps, clustered column charts, risk matrices, and KPI cards to highlight critical details and emphasize high-risk segments. Additionally, I integrated customer lifetime value (CLV) to identify customers who, despite high risk, represent significant future value to the business.
  • Throughout the project, I communicated findings clearly to support data-driven decision-making and help stakeholders easily grasp the key insights. Based on this analysis, I developed actionable recommendations to mitigate customer default risk and improve retention strategies.
